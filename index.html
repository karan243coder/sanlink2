MeetLink Terms & Privacy

Last Updated: July 5, 2026

1. End-To-End Video Call

By connecting to calls in MeetLink, you agree that your video call stream is not recorded and is never uploaded to any administrator's or any external service. Your calls remain private.

2. Privacy & Data Protection

MeetLink does not collect or store your personal information. No identity tracking, analytics, or cookie-based tracking is used. All peer connections operate securely through standard WebRTC frameworks.

3. Secure File Sharing

Files shared during calls are transferred directly between connected devices using encrypted peer-to-peer (P2P) channels and are not stored on any server.
